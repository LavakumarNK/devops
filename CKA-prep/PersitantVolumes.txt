PV: persistant volume
PVC: Persistance volume claim 
=====================================

PV and PVC are two diffrent objects , PV's are created by Administartor where as PVC's are created by users 

PV and PVC, There is a one to one relationship between PV and PVC, if there no PV available to PVC then PVC remain pending once the PV available the it will automatically bind with it.
